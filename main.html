Inception is a science fiction thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for performing the seemingly impossible task of "inception": planting an idea into a target's mind.
